# Cohort Definition

## Working Scope

All oncology antibody-drug conjugates (ADCs) that received FDA approval on or before the frozen study cutoff date.

## Cutoff Date

Working cutoff date: 2026-05-31.

This date should be verified against current FDA records before protocol freeze.

## Inclusion Criteria

- Antibody-drug conjugate used for oncology indications.
- Received at least one FDA approval on or before the cutoff date.
- Includes products that are currently approved, withdrawn, converted from accelerated to regular approval, or reapproved.
- Includes hematologic malignancy and solid tumor indications.

## Exclusion Criteria

- Non-oncology ADCs.
- Radioimmunoconjugates without a cytotoxic payload-linker ADC structure unless specifically justified.
- Investigational ADCs without FDA approval by the cutoff date.

## Analysis Levels

1. Drug level.
2. FDA approval-event level.
3. Pivotal-trial level.
4. Treatment-arm level.
5. Adverse-event concept level.
6. Source-document level.
