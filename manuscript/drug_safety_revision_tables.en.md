## Cross-source safety outcome comparability criteria

| Dimension | Primary comparable | Sensitivity only | Non-comparable |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Trial | Same trial | Not applicable | Different trials |
| Treatment arm/dose | Same treatment arm and dose cohort | Documented pooling or cohort alignment with the same ADC regimen | Different treatment arms or dose cohorts |
| Analysis population | Same safety population | Trial-specific population compared with a documented broader regulatory safety population | Clearly different or insufficiently described populations |
| Denominator | Identical or inferably identical | Documented denominator difference with same arm and interpretable population relationship | Unexplained or large denominator difference |
| Safety concept | Same aggregate safety concept | Closely related but not identical concept | Different safety concepts |
| Grade/seriousness | Same grade or seriousness definition | Definition not fully explicit but no identified conflicting grade or seriousness rule | Grade >=3 AE mixed with serious AE or incompatible definition |
| Causality | Both all-cause or both treatment-related | Broader causality category with clear metadata | All-cause directly compared with treatment-related without alignment |
| Observation window | Same cutoff or clearly aligned safety window | Window not fully explicit but no identified conflicting cutoff | Clearly different or unalignable reporting windows |
